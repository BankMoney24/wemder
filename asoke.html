Aso Oke

The Aso Oke Collection

Woven with tradition, worn with pride. Experience Wemder's finest.

Step into a world of unparalleled elegance with Wemder's Aso Oke collection. Aso Oke, the "prestige cloth" of the Yoruba people, is more than just fabric; it's a statement of cultural heritage, artistry, and timeless sophistication. Each piece is meticulously hand-woven by master artisans, carrying stories of tradition and celebration in every thread.

Rich Heritage

Our Aso Oke embodies centuries of Yoruba weaving tradition, bringing authentic cultural significance to your special occasions.

Master Craftsmanship

Each thread is woven with precision and care by skilled artisans, ensuring a luxurious feel and exceptional durability.

Timeless Elegance

From classic Sanyan and vibrant Alaari to deep Etu, discover Aso Oke that defines prestige for weddings, chieftaincies, and celebrations.

Wemder offers a curated selection of Aso Oke, perfect for creating breathtaking Agbada, Iro and Buba, Gele, and Fila. Whether for a wedding, festival, or a significant cultural event, our Aso Oke ensures you stand out with dignity and unmatched style. Embrace the legacy, wear Wemder.
